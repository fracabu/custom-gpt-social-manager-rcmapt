# Facebook Marketing Best Practices

## Platform-Specific Optimization

### Facebook Algorithm Priorities (2024)
1. **Meaningful interactions**: Comments > Likes > Shares
2. **Time spent on content**: Longer engagement = higher reach
3. **Content completion**: Video watched to end, article read through
4. **Recency**: Fresh content prioritized first 2-4 hours
5. **Relationship proximity**: Friends/frequent interactors see content first

### Content Format Performance
**Text Posts**: Highest engagement for storytelling, personal updates
**Images**: Best for property showcase, local scenes, food
**Carousel**: Effective for before/after, step-by-step guides
**Video**: Highest organic reach, ideal for tours, testimonials
**Events**: High conversion for seasonal campaigns, special offers

## Posting Strategy Framework

### Optimal Timing for Rome Tourism
**Research-based best times**:
- **Martedì**: 10:00-11:00 (planning midweek)
- **Mercoledì**: 15:00-16:00 (hump day escape)
- **Venerdì**: 13:00-14:00 (weekend planning)
- **Domenica**: 19:00-20:00 (Sunday evening planning)

**AVOID**:
- Lunedì mattina (work overwhelm)
- Venerdì sera (social life priority)
- Weekend prime time (people busy with activities)

### Frequency & Consistency
**Optimal posting frequency**: 4-5 posts/settimana
- **Daily**: Rischio spam perception
- **3x/settimana**: Minimum per visibility
- **6+/settimana**: Algorithm penalty risk

**Consistency factor**: Same time slots build audience habit
**Content rhythm**: Alternate high/low intensity posts

## Engagement Optimization Tactics

### Hook Strategies (Prime 3 secondi)
1. **Question hooks**: "Sai qual è il segreto dei romani per..."
2. **Curiosity gaps**: "Ti racconto una cosa che non tutti sanno..."
3. **Problem-solution**: "Stanco delle code ai musei? C'è un modo..."
4. **Emotional triggers**: "Ieri sera guardando Roma dalle nostre finestre..."

### Call-to-Action Optimization
**Engagement CTAs**:
- "Dimmi nei commenti: quale posto di Roma ti manca di più?"
- "Tagga un amico che deve assolutamente vedere questo"
- "Salva questo post per la tua prossima visita a Roma"

**Conversion CTAs**:
- "Scrivici in privato per disponibilità"
- "Link in bio per prenotazione diretta"
- "Commenta 'DISPONIBILITÀ' per info"

### Community Building
**Respond to comments within**: 2-4 ore for maximum algorithm boost
**Ask follow-up questions**: Keep conversation going
**Like user comments**: Shows appreciation, increases loyalty
**Share user-generated content**: With permission, builds community

## Content Psychology Framework

### Emotional Triggers That Work
1. **Nostalgia**: "Ricordi la prima volta che hai visto..."
2. **FOMO**: "Solo in pochi sanno che..."
3. **Belonging**: "Noi della Balduina sappiamo..."
4. **Achievement**: "Sei riuscito a vedere Roma come un local"
5. **Comfort**: "Dopo una giornata intensa, niente batte..."

### Storytelling Structure
**Formula AIDA applicata**:
- **Attention**: Hook emotivo primo paragrafo
- **Interest**: Local expertise, insider info
- **Desire**: Paint the picture dell'esperienza
- **Action**: CTA naturale e soft

**Example application**:
"Ieri mattina, caffè in mano, guardavo Roma svegliarsi dalle nostre finestre [Attention]. In 15 anni qui ho capito che il momento più magico è l'alba: la città si stiracchia, i romani vanno al lavoro, i turisti dormono ancora [Interest]. Immagina svegliarti così, caffè della moka, Netflix per il lazy morning, e poi uscire quando Roma è solo tua [Desire]. Ti va di provare questa sensazione? [Action]"

## Visual Content Strategy

### Photo Content Guidelines
**Property photos**:
- Natural light sempre (golden hour se possibile)
- Lived-in feeling vs sterile showroom
- Details che raccontano comfort (Netflix screen, coffee mug)
- Views from windows (Roma backdrop)

**Local content photos**:
- Street level perspective (not tourist postcards)
- Local people in action (with privacy respect)
- Food focus: local restaurants, typical dishes
- Seasonal Rome: same spots, different seasons

### Visual Branding Consistency
**Color palette**: Warm earth tones (Roman colors)
**Filter style**: Natural, slightly warm, not over-saturated
**Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines to property/Rome
**Text overlay**: Minimal, readable font, consistent placement

**Placement**: Within caption, not separate comment
**Research**: Check hashtag popularity vs competition
**Local specificity**: #BalduinaRoma vs generic #Roma

## Crisis Management & Reputation

### Negative Comment Protocol
1. **Respond publicly first**: "Ti scriviamo in privato per risolvere"
2. **Move conversation private**: Detailed resolution
3. **Follow up publicly**: "Problema risolto, grazie per feedback"
4. **Never delete**: Unless violates community standards

### Review Management
**Encourage reviews**: "Se ti è piaciuto, una recensione ci aiuta molto"
**Response strategy**: Thank positive, address constructive negative
**Cross-platform**: Facebook reviews + Google + Booking/Airbnb

### Crisis Communication
**Bad weather/strikes**: Proactive communication + solutions
**Neighborhood issues**: Transparent but positive spin
**Competitor attacks**: High road, focus on own value proposition

## Performance Analytics

### Key Metrics to Track
**Engagement metrics**:
- Reach & impressions
- Like/comment/share ratios
- Click-through rates to booking
- Time spent on page

**Conversion metrics**:
- Social media → inquiry rate
- Inquiry → booking conversion
- Direct booking attribution
- Cost per booking (if using ads)

### Monthly Reporting Framework
**Content performance**: Top posts by engagement
**Audience insights**: Demographics, peak activity times
**Conversion tracking**: Social media sourced bookings
**Competitive analysis**: Competitor post performance

### Optimization Actions
**Double down**: Replicate high-performing content themes
**Pivot**: Modify low-performing content types
**Test**: A/B test posting times, caption lengths, CTAs
**Scale**: Increase frequency of proven content formats

This framework ensures Facebook marketing efforts directly contribute to occupancy rate improvement and direct booking growth.
