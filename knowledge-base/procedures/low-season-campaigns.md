## Offer Structure & Psychology

### Pricing Psychology
**EVITA**: "Desperate" pricing (-50%, "emergency deals")
**USA**: "Value enhancement" (same quality, special price)
**FRAME**: "Seasonal advantage" non "low season discount"

### Offer Types Effectiveness
1. **Bundle value**: "Include qualcosa gratis" vs discount diretto
2. **Time-limited**: "Solo questa settimana" vs "sempre"
3. **Exclusive**: "Per chi ci segue" vs "per tutti"
4. **Experience**: "Roma d'inverno" vs "prezzo basso"
