# Project Overview - Roma Caput Mundi Social Media Manager

## Executive Summary

This Custom GPT system is designed to automate and optimize social media marketing for **"Roma Caput Mundi Apartment"** - a premium vacation rental in Rome's Balduina district. The system generates strategic Facebook content that drives direct bookings, fills low-season gaps, and positions the property as the preferred choice for authentic Roman experiences.

## Business Context

### Property Profile
- **Location**: Balduina district, 100m from Appiano FS station, 2 stops to Vatican
- **Property**: 50m² bilocale with premium services (Netflix, Sky, fast WiFi)
- **Target Market**: Italian tourists (70%) and international visitors (30%)
- **Guest Profile**: Couples (60%), families (30%), solo travelers (10%)
- **Stay Duration**: 1-6 nights, average 2-3 nights

### Current Performance (5-Month Analysis)
- **Revenue**: €10,571 total (€2,208/month average)
- **Occupancy**: 64% (vs 72-77% market average)
- **Average Rate**: €114.98/night (18-42% below market potential)
- **Booking Distribution**: 79.4% Booking.com, 20.6% Airbnb
- **Commission Cost**: €2,086 (18.8% of revenue)

### Strategic Objectives
1. **Increase Direct Bookings**: Reduce OTA dependency and 18-20% commission fees
2. **Fill Low-Season Gaps**: Target January-February and November gaps
3. **Optimize Pricing**: Move toward market rates through value demonstration
4. **Enhance Occupancy**: Reach 72-77% occupancy rate benchmark
5. **Revenue Growth**: Achieve 22.6%-73.1% revenue increase potential
